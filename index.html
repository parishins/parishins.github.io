About Me

Hey! I’m a passionate and curious Mechanical Engineering student who finds joy in blending logic with creativity. I believe in problem-solving creativity. Whether I’m designing robotic arms, sketching CAD models, or diving into research on bioinspired automation, I love exploring how machines can move with grace and purpose. Beyond engineering, I adore the arts — painting delicate scenes, singing softly in cozy spaces, and dancing to my own rhythm. I believe in lifelong learning, gentle persistence, and creating with both heart and mind.

Thank you for visiting my space — I hope my work reflects the same warmth and elegance I strive for in life.
